Why Staking ETFs and ETPs?

Staking ETFs and ETPs offer users an easy and secure way to participate in the lucrative staking ecosystem without the complexities of managing individual assets. Unlike spot products, which only track the price of the underlying asset, staking ETFs/ETPs provide users with the ability to earn rewards (APR) from staking directly, all within a convenient investment vehicle. This can unlock higher returns and increased exposure to decentralized finance protocols, which are not available with traditional spot trading.

Spot Products vs Staked Products: While spot products focus on price appreciation, staked products give users the added benefit of earning annual staking rewards. The result is a dual opportunity for growth: the potential upside from the asset's price and the additional yield from staking rewards, making staking ETFs/ETPs a compelling choice for investors seeking both growth and income.
