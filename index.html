Why Staking ETFs and ETPs?

Staking ETFs and ETPs provide an easy and secure way to participate in staking, offering both price appreciation and annual staking rewards. Compared to traditional spot products, these ETFs/ETPs deliver yield from staking without requiring users to manage individual assets.
